Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the OPD timings?

Our General OPD is open from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM and 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM (Mon-Sat). Emergency services are available 24/7.

Do you accept Ayushman Bharat cards?

Yes, we are a fully authorized hospital for Ayushman Bharat Yojana. Please bring your card and Aadhar card for cashless treatment.

Is cashless insurance available?

Yes, we have tie-ups with all major TPA and insurance companies including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, SBI General, and ICICI Lombard.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book by calling +91 90090 99380, using the WhatsApp button on this website, or visiting the reception desk directly.
